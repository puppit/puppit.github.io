PUPPit

VISION

The main goal of the workshops is not only to pass the knowledge of building and manipulating a puppet, but also to introduce our students into the world of scenic arts and mutual collaboration. We focus on creativity and imagination along the process, where the students can freely develop and capture their own ideas into the puppets and their personalities.

We offer different workshops depending on our student goals, and every workshop can be customizable depending on the skills, age and time the student has to spare. This is precisely why we don’t necessarily offer a stiff program, cause our main goal is for them to have fun, develop communicational-improvising skills and getting them involved into the amazing world of puppeteering.


BIO

Sebastian Bluck

I’m a Professional Actor graduated from Universidad Finnis Terrae (Chile) the year 2015 with a Direction Diploma on 2016. That same year I joined a theater company called “Paopao productions”, wich focuses on kids theater and learning programs/activities for them. In that company I met my puppet master Freddy Espindola, who introduced me into the world of puppeteering. From then on, I’ve been doing and teaching puppets to everyone who’s interested in this beautiful art. The year 2016 I started an afterschool workshop for kids, having more than 15 permanent students and much more of them that learned the fun art of puppeteering in the workshop.

Along my professional career I’ve performed on stages as an actor, director and teacher, however my pedagogic vocation has always been my north, developing an approach to push the students towards their strengths and concerns, so they follow their own goals and interests as long as they acquire the required skills.



WORKSHOPS

All the workshops are recommended for people from 6 years old and include all the materials needed. They can be done by English or Spanish speakers. In case (for example) a father and his daughter want to do the workshop, but the kid doesn’t speak neither language, depending on the situation we can figure something out.

Besides both of the workshops, if the students want to keep learning more deeply how to handle/create different kind of puppets, or techniques regarding the world of puppeteering, we can create a particular workshop that focuses more accurately on the student needs. This can go from improvisation classes to recycled materials puppet building. We prefer to talk directly with our future students this subject, because everyone has different needs, and our idea is to pass this knowledge the more efficient and fun way possible.

In all of the courses we supply all the materials needed for the construction of the puppet, and also a small bibliography for everyone who wants to go a little bit further on their own. We also highly recommend that the workshop have at least two students, cause that way they will acquire more efficiently the skills that we want to teach

As we are not into getting rich doing this, we usually ask our students to do some homework depending on the workshop they designed, so the classes are not only focused on sewing or stitching the puppet. This way the student will have more time to play/improvise, which translate into a more efficient learning process


BUILDING WORKSHOP

The building workshop counts of 4 classes of 3 hours, where the student will learn how to design and build their own puppets. The student will also learn basic notions on puppet handling, improvisation and character creation. The price of this workshop is 120 euros/student.


PUPPETEERING WORKSHOP

The puppeteering workshop counts of 4 more classes of 3 hours that focuses on a more complete understanding of the puppeteer, where the student will go through exercises that will help him develop more refined skills to handle and create his puppet character. The student will also learn basic notions of theater and improvisation theory, which will help him to develop more complex performances during the exercises.
